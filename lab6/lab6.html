Lab 6 Favicon, banner, and branding

October. 14, 2021
Faria Hossain

In lab 6 we learn how to create our own logo and favicon by using pixels and codes. Favicons are small images which identify the website in a browser. In order to create a favicon you must first get the image you would like to input,I created my favicon by using binary codes. We then input the image in our image into our website and add some HTML codes. We use a website called code.org. We started off by creating our logo on graph paper and then transferred it digitally to simple black and white pixels. We then learned about making colors through hexadecimals/codes and made our design in color. After that, we designed our own banner for our webpage. We put it all together by adding images describing how our design looks on paper, color, and in black and white.
